Transparency & Quality

Our Production & Quality Control

Every product goes through our rigorous 4-stage QC system. Full traceability from raw material to shipment with 20+ tests per batch and 99.8% pass rate.

Production Process

1

Raw Material Inspection (IQC)

All PET/HDPE resin tested for food-grade compliance before entering production.

2

In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)

Real-time monitoring of temperature, pressure, and dimensions during molding.

3

Final Quality Control (FQC)

100% visual inspection + AQL sampling for dimensions, weight, and seal integrity.

4

Outgoing Quality Assurance (OQC)

Final check before shipment — packaging, labeling, and documentation verification.
